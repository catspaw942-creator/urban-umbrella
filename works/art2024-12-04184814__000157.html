In the ruins of the city, Lina was no longer the helpless student she once was.

Now, she was a well-known mercenary. Armed with a heavy machine gun and riding a compact military motorcycle,

her cute cat ears and tail had become her signature. She took on all kinds of missions—escorting civilians, raiding enemy bases—with precision and determination, never wasting time on hesitation.

Yet, after every mission, she would pause for a moment, standing amidst the rubble. Her eyes scanned the desolate streets, as if searching for something lost—a faint trace of hope.
